## What is Yumii?

Yumii is an open-source AI companion that runs on your own machine. She
listens through your microphone, thinks with the LLM provider you choose,
answers out loud in a fully **local voice**, and shows up on your desktop as a
small floating orb — always there, never in the way.

She is built around three promises:

* **Voice-first.** Real conversation, not a chat box. You can interrupt her
  mid-sentence and she stops and listens, the way a person would.
* **Private by design.** Conversations, memory, and API keys live in local
  files on your disk. The only network traffic is to the AI providers you
  explicitly configure. Her voice is synthesized locally on your CPU.
* **She asks before she acts.** Yumii can use tools — read your email, search
  the web — but every outside action goes through a permission prompt on your
  screen first. You approve or deny, every time, until you decide otherwise.

## What she can do today

* **Real-time voice conversation** with barge-in interruption and roughly a
  second of reply latency on an ordinary CPU.
* **Six personalities** — caring, tsundere, genki, kuudere, yandere, dandere —
  switchable mid-conversation by just asking.
* **Long-term memory.** She extracts facts about you from conversation and
  brings them up naturally in later sessions. You can browse, edit, and delete
  everything she knows from the dashboard.
* **Tools with a permission gate.** Web search out of the box; Gmail and other
  real apps via a [Composio](https://composio.dev) account.
* **A dashboard** for settings, API keys, past conversations (with readable
  transcripts), and memory.
* **Local text-to-speech** (Kokoro-82M) bundled in the installer — her voice
  works offline, on the first run, with nothing to download.
