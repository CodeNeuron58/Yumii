## What is Yumi?

Yumi is an interactive, real-time AI waifu designed to act as your virtual companion. 
With dynamic, expressive visuals and an intelligent conversational backend, Yumi provides 
a highly engaging and responsive experience.

Unlike sterile utility tools, Yumi is built with a focus on **presence**. She listens, reacts 
dynamically, and synchronizes her facial expressions to the emotional tone of her thoughts.

## Key Features

<FeatureList cols={2}>
  <DocsCard title="Real-time Voice Recognition" icon="waveform-lines">
    Captures and processes your voice seamlessly using local, low-latency Whisper models.
  </DocsCard>
  <DocsCard title="Lifelike Voice Synthesis" icon="face-smile">
    Utilizes ElevenLabs TTS for incredibly realistic, high-quality, and expressive voice generation.
  </DocsCard>
  <DocsCard title="Dynamic Visuals" icon="cube">
    Brought to life using HTML5, WebSockets, and Live2D, Yumi reacts to your conversations with fluid body movements.
  </DocsCard>
  <DocsCard title="Emotional Intelligence" icon="rocket">
    Powered by LLMs (Groq, OpenAI, Anthropic), Yumi's expressions dynamically change based on the emotional context of the dialogue.
  </DocsCard>
  <DocsCard title="Interactive Terminal Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
    A beautiful, fully interactive CLI to configure and launch her easily.
  </DocsCard>
</FeatureList>
